About Me

My name is Ethan Bernich. I am a sophomore at the University of New Hampshire studying Finance and Economics. I recently transferred from RIT, where I studied Economics and played collegiate lacrosse.

I was born in Long Island, New York in 2006 before moving to Exeter, New Hampshire in 2020.

I am passionate about my studies here at UNH, and am involved in various clubs on campus. I participate in Atkins Investment Group, Men's Club Lacrosse, Economics Club, and Outing Club.

Outside of my academics, I am incredibly passionate about music, and recording and performing music with my band, the Lost River Fleet. I also love lifeguarding in the summers at Hampton Beach, and love surfing year round.
